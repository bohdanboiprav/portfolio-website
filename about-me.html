WORLD OF PHOTOGRAPHY AND VIDEOGRAPHY

Photography and videography are more than just hobbies for me—they're my life's passion and a journey of self-discovery. From my early days as a social media manager at the young age of 14, I've always been immersed in the digital world. But it was my love for exploration and travel that truly opened my eyes to the beauty around me. Over the past few years, I've traveled distances equivalent to circling the equator twice, drawing inspiration from every corner of the globe.

In May 2019, something clicked within me as I delved into the works of fellow creators. It was then that I realized my desire to capture the world's wonders through my lens. With the purchase of my first camera, my life took a dramatic turn, guiding me towards new opportunities and connections with like-minded individuals.

The past year has been a whirlwind of photoshoots and video projects, each one pushing me to refine my skills and embrace my creative vision. Participating in projects like "Svoe kino" has not only expanded my portfolio but has also provided me with invaluable experiences and lessons.

For me, photography and videography are more than just capturing moments—they're a means of storytelling. With every click of the shutter and frame of footage, I strive to weave narratives that evoke emotions and inspire others to see the world in a new light. My journey as a photographer and videographer is a testament to the transformative power of art and the boundless possibilities that lie behind the lens.
